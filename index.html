RPA · Meta Platforms

68,000 hours saved annually

Problem

Manual processes across Meta's internal systems were consuming significant team capacity, with high potential for human error.

Built

Led a cross-functional team to deliver a UiPath bot integrated seamlessly with Meta's internal systems, and trained end-users to operate and maintain it.

Result: 68,000 hours saved annually, reduced manual errors
n8n · Accenture

Autonomous operations agent

Problem

Customer-facing operations teams were spending excessive time manually classifying and resolving common order issues.

Built

An n8n-based operational agent that classifies and resolves common order issues autonomously, alongside a RAG chatbot that schedules sales meetings from Facebook leads.

Result: 65% of customer-facing ops tasks automated
Data Engineering · Thakral One

Unified customer data pipeline

Problem

Customer attributes were fragmented across Cards, Deposits, Customer, and Digital domains with no unified structure for integration.

Built

Designed and maintained a Single Customer View mapping file, with automated data quality checks using Soda to ensure accuracy and campaign readiness.

Result: streamlined integration across all customer touchpoints
Analytics · S&P Global Platts

Real-time NPS resolution system

Problem

Customer feedback themes were difficult to categorize at scale, slowing down resolution and obscuring root-cause patterns.

Built

A Net Promoter Score case management system with fuzzy matching and topic modeling to categorize key feedback themes in real time.

Result: real-time resolution for 1,000+ client concerns
